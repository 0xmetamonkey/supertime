# Supertime Pricing Strategy ("Growth Gateway Model")

*Last Updated: Pre-Launch Strategy Phase*

**Core Philosophy:** Supertime should ONLY make money when the creator makes money. Platform fees should never be a source of stress for a creator trying to build their business. We give them generous free tools (like 2k Auto DMs) to guarantee their success, and only charge a subscription when they have scaled to a point where the subscription cost is mathematically negligible compared to their earnings.

## Tier 1: Supertime Basic (Indie Tier)
**Target:** New creators building an audience.
**Price:** $0/month + 10% Transaction Fee.

*   **1:1 Bookings:** Unlimited Scheduled Calls (Using Supertime Native Video).
*   **Broadcasting:** 1:1 Live Shows (Creator sets the price/min).
*   **Instagram Auto-DMs:** Up to 2,000 automated DMs per month.
*   **Digital Products:** Sell unlimited files & links.
*   **Economics:** If we capture 100 creators filling 120 mins a day on this tier, the 10% rolling commission generates massive revenue for the platform while costing the creator $0 upfront.

## Tier 2: Supertime Pro (Scale Tier)
**Target:** Established creators hitting infrastructure caps.
**Price:** $29/month (or similar) + 0% Transaction Fee (or heavily reduced).

*   **Why they upgrade:** They are consistently capping out their 2,000 Auto-DMs and need more volume to capture leads, OR they are processing so many paid bookings/sales that the $29 flat fee is *cheaper* for them than paying the 10% commission on the Free tier.
*   **Features Unlocked:**
    *   Unlimited Instagram Auto-DMs.
    *   0% Platform Commission (Only standard PG fees apply like Razorpay 2%).
    *   Advanced Analytics & Exportable Fan Emails.
    *   Custom Domain Routing (e.g., `links.creator.com` instead of `supertime.wtf/creator`).
*   **Economics:** This is where the predictable MRR comes in, built entirely on creators who are *already winning*.

## Tier 3: Supertime Partners (Custom Tier)
**Target:** High-ticket creators, Brands, Agencies.
**Price:** Custom Pricing ($499/mo or equivalent).

*   **Why they upgrade:** They need white-glove setup or dedicated account management to handle massive volume.

---

## The "Failsafe" Logic (Why this works):
1.  **Lowering the Barrier:** Creators hate subscriptions. By making all features (Calls, DMs, Store) free to start, the barrier to try Supertime is zero.
2.  **Guaranteed ROI:** By the time a creator hits the 2k DM cap, they *should* be generating sales. If they are generating sales, they are happy to pay $29/mo to keep the machine running.
3.  **Risk Mitigation:** Ensure backend infrastructure (like Agora video minutes) operates at a lower cost than the minimum transaction fee generated by the bookings, ensuring free-tier calls don't bleed platform cash.
