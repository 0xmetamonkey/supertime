# Supertime Roadmap

## Phase 1: Core Platform Stabilization & Subscription Infrastructure (Completed)
- Clean up architecture and streamline routing.
- Integrate real-time Agora calling and booking systems.
- Launch "Inner Circle" subscriptions with Razorpay handling 30-day access passes.
- Integrate a simple markdown-powered "Feast" editorial tab to lock content behind subscriptions.

## Phase 2: The Studio Engine & Free Transcription (Completed)
- Implement persistent global recording (floating studio widget) on the dashboard.
- Integrate 100% free, real-time transcription using the native Web Speech API.
- Seamless auto-publishing of audio webms/mp4s to the Feast timeline.
- Implement "Extended Minutes" paywall (Free users capped at 60 seconds audio preview; Subscribers get unlimited listening).
- Cross-platform compatibility improvements (iOS Safari MediaRecorder mimeType handling).

## Phase 3: The Supertime NFT Engine & DAO Governance (Upcoming)
> "Each and every moment recorded on supertime is an asset."

- **Feast-to-NFT Minting:** Enable creators to seamlessly mint their audio/text feasts as non-fungible tokens (NFTs).
- **Asset Monetization:** Allow fans to collect specific Feasts, calls, or recordings, generating secondary revenue for creators.
- **On-chain Reputation:** Tie Inner Circle subscriptions or high-value collectors to on-chain badges that appear on their profile.
- **Decentralized Storage:** Explore mirroring audio blobs and transcripts to IPFS/Arweave for permanent immutable storage of creator assets.

### 🗳️ Active DAO Governance Proposals
*These agendas should be discussed openly between human DAO members. We will set up a governance portal (e.g., Snapshot or Discourse) where members can vote with their $TIME tokens.*
- **Proposal 1: What do we call our fans?** Patrons? Admirers? Kadradan? Believers? Need community consensus to standardize the language across the platform.
- **Proposal 2: Feature Prioritization.** Which NFT mechanic should be built first?
- **Proposal 3: Platform Fees.** How should the DAO treasury be funded from transactions?

## Phase 4: Discovery & The "Explore" Page (Future)
- Showcase top creator sessions (1:1 calls).
- Highlight trending Art & Products.
- Feature trending "Inner Circle" creator subscriptions.
- Future addition: Live Shows section highlighting currently broadcasting creators.
